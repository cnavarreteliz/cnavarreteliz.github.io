About me

Hola! My name is Carlos Navarrete. I am Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the Université de Toulouse. Today, I am affiliated to the School of Engineering at the University of Concepción. I conduct research at the intersection of computer and political science, aiming to understand the dynamics of agreements, disagreements, and polarization in society through computational techniques.

My Ph.D. research was carried out at the Institut de Recherche en Informatique de Toulouse (IRIT) at the University of Toulouse, at the Department of Human Geography and Spatial Planning at Utrecht University, and at the Unidad de Data Science at the Universidad de Concepción.

I have participated as a web developer and researcher in several digital projects around the world, such as Chilecracia, DataChile, and Mon Programme 2022, to name a few.
